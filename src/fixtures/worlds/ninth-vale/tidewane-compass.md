The Tidewane Compass surfaced during the founding tides, long before the [[cartographers-guild]] kept records precise enough to say where it came from. [[sera]] inherited it from her mother, who never explained how she came by it, and has carried it on every survey since.

Everything the guild's private notes agree on:

1. The needle does not point north. It points toward the nearest gap in whatever chart is being held.
2. It grows warm to the touch near [[highland-stair]], for reasons no apprentice has explained.
3. It has never, in Sera's records, pointed toward the same gap twice, except once: toward the [[the-ninth-map]], every single season since the drowning years began.

Sera does not discuss the third finding with the [[order-ember]], and would prefer this fixture not either.
