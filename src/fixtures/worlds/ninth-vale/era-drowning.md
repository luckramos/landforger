The Drowning Years opened with the [[siege-duskwater]] and did not meaningfully close for a generation. The [[hollow-king]] is first recorded in this era. The [[order-ember]] rose to power in it, on the promise of ending it.

> The tide does not forgive a coastline for what it forgets to chart. — attributed to the guild's own Warden of the Hall, era unknown, quoted in every apprentice's first lesson since

[[ashthorn-keep]] fell during this era's opening siege and never fully recovered its garrison. [[emberglass]] seams found along the keep's foundations are dated to this same stretch of years.
