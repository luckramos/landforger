Before the drowning years, this was simply Duskwater's low quarter — tanneries, a rope-walk, the cheaper end of the [[harbor-gate]] approach. It earned its current name honestly, one flood-year at a time, until even the guild's ledgers stopped calling it anything else.

The [[hollow-king]] has been sighted here more than once, always at the tideline, always gone by the time anyone reliable arrives to look. The fishwives who still work the quarter's upper streets have their own theories about why, and the [[cartographers-guild]] has learned not to argue with them.
