The Harbor Gate marks the one approach to [[duskwater]] that the tide has never fully closed, which makes it the single most useful landmark on every chart the [[cartographers-guild]] has produced since the founding. It has been rebuilt three times, each time a little higher, each time on the same footprint.

[[sera]] anchors most of her harbor surveys from the gate's inner arch. During the drowning years it also served, briefly and unofficially, as the last dry ground for anyone fleeing the [[siege-duskwater]] — a fact the guild's official history omits and everyone who lived through it remembers anyway.
