Emberglass forms where the fire of [[the-sundering]] met the tide head-on and neither one won cleanly — a black-orange glass, warm to the touch centuries later, found in seams along the foundations of [[ashthorn-keep]]. The [[order-ember]] collects every shard it can find and burns what it can't collect.

The [[cartographers-guild]] marks emberglass seams on its charts anyway, over the Order's objections, on the grounds that a coastline can't be honestly mapped with holes cut out of it.
