The Order of the Ember rose to authority over the coast in the years after [[the-sundering]], on the promise that the old magic which caused the cataclysm would be found and extinguished wherever it still smoldered. In practice, that promise has meant burning charts, records, and eventually entire archives the Order decides are too dangerous, or too inconvenient, to leave standing.

The [[cartographers-guild]] has survived the Order's attention so far by being useful — someone has to draw the coast the Order's own surveyors are too frightened of the [[hollow-king]] to approach. [[sera]] does not trust that arrangement to last.
