The Salt Market climbs the terraces above [[duskwater]]'s worst flooding, high enough that it has only gone under twice in living memory — both times during the drowning years, and both times it reopened within the season. Salt, dried fish, and increasingly the salvage trade all move through its stalls.

When the market itself floods, trade shifts wholesale to the great hall of [[ashthorn-keep]] — see [[keep-hall]] — a fact the guild considers charming and the merchants consider an outrage they repeat every single time.
