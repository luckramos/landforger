Keep Court sits directly below [[keep-tower]], flagstones cracked in a pattern the guild has surveyed twice and drawn twice, differently, both times. [[corin]] says he last saw the [[hollow-king]] stand here long enough to count the cracks himself, though he has never agreed to say what number he reached.
