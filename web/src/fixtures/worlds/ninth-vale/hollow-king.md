No one alive crowned the Hollow King, and no one alive can say with certainty that he still needs to be. He surfaced in the vale during [[era-drowning]], wearing a crown no herald in [[duskwater]] recognizes and answering to a title no genealogy in the [[cartographers-guild]]'s archive explains.

What is agreed on, by everyone from the [[order-ember]] down to the fishwives of the [[drowned-quarter]], is this: wherever he walks, the tide follows within the season, and it does not recede on schedule afterward.

- He has been sighted at [[highland-stair]] on three separate flood-years, always alone.
- He has never been recorded speaking a full sentence to anyone who lived to transcribe it.
- He is, by every account, patient in a way that unsettles even [[corin]].

[[sera]] does not believe he is a person in any sense the guild's forms have a field for. She has stopped trying to file a report on him and started, instead, simply mapping where he has been.
