The Salt & Cinder Days are the coast's current, uneasy present: the tide still rising, the [[order-ember]] still burning what it decides is dangerous, and the [[cartographers-guild]] still surveying anyway. [[sera]]'s search for [[the-ninth-map]] belongs entirely to this era — see [[salt-cinder]].

Most of the world's active pages — [[duskwater]], the [[salt-market]], the [[drowned-quarter]] — are pinned to this era on the map, alongside whichever earlier eras they've survived from.
