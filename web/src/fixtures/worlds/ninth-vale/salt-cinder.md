## The season the compass stopped changing its mind

[[sera]] has spent three seasons chasing what the [[tidewane-compass]] keeps pointing toward: a gap in every chart the [[cartographers-guild]] has ever drawn, in exactly the place where [[the-ninth-map]] is rumored to have survived [[the-sundering]]. The [[order-ember]] would prefer the rumor, and the gap, stay exactly where they are — unexamined.

[[corin]] has attached himself to the search on no authority but his own, which suits Sera fine until his old enemies at [[ashthorn-keep]] start taking an interest in why an exiled knight is suddenly walking the [[ninth-vale]] again with a purpose. Meanwhile something that may or may not be the [[hollow-king]] has started leaving the coastline slightly different every time Sera returns to re-survey it — which is either encouragement or a warning, and she has stopped being sure which.

Sera's working expedition checklist, pinned above her desk at the [[guild-hall]]:

- [x] Chart the shoal past [[highland-stair]]
- [x] Confirm the compass reading against three independent surveys
- [ ] Recover the Ninth Map, or proof it never existed
- [ ] Return to Duskwater before the Order notices what she's been asking

She has never crossed out the third line. She has crossed out worse.
