The Cartographers' Guild has surveyed the coast since before anyone thought to call the eras by name, working out of the [[guild-hall]] above [[duskwater]]'s harbor. Its charter is simple and, these days, quietly dangerous: draw the coastline as it is, not as the [[order-ember]] would prefer it recorded.

The guild's duties, as the charter still lists them:

- Survey the coast every season, without exception, regardless of who objects.
- Maintain the archive at the [[guild-hall]] against fire, flood, and the Order's occasional "requests."
- Train apprentices in the old measuring methods, not the Order-approved shortcuts.

:::toggle {summary="Guild Ranks"}

Apprentice, then Third Rank, then Second, then First, then Warden of the Hall. [[sera]] holds Third Rank and has, by her own account, no intention of pursuing Second — the examiners' notes on her charts make the reason clear enough.

:::

Mention the [[the-ninth-map]] inside the guild hall and you will get three different answers about whether it ever existed, and one look from whoever is closest to the door checking who else heard you ask.
