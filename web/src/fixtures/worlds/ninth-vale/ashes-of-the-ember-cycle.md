This one is still mostly notes. What exists so far follows [[corin]] through the last week of the [[siege-duskwater]], up to the gate of [[ashthorn-keep]] opening from the inside — and stops there, because no draft yet agrees on who, or what, opened it.

The working theory, scrawled in the margin of the guild's copy: it was never a person at gate at all. The [[hollow-king]] is mentioned nowhere in any record before that night. Every record after treats him as simply a fact of the coast.

Whether that theory survives to a finished draft depends, mostly, on whether Corin ever agrees to be interviewed about it directly rather than at three drinks' remove.
