Keep Hall was built to seat the garrison of [[ashthorn-keep]] at table; it now more often seats the [[salt-market]]'s overflow trade whenever the terraces flood. [[corin]] avoids it when he can — he held the gate that opens off this hall for eleven years, and the room remembers him whether he wants it to or not.
