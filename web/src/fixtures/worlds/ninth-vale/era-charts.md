The Age of Charts takes its name from the [[cartographers-guild]]'s founding, some generations after [[the-sundering]] settled into a coastline people could plan around. [[duskwater]] grew into a proper city in this era; the [[guild-hall]] and much of the [[harbor-gate]] date from it.

[[sera]] was trained in this era's methods, at the [[guild-hall]], and still keeps them even now that [[era-drowning]] proved the coast was never as settled as the Age of Charts believed.
