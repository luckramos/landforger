# Before the Waters Rose

No two accounts of the Sundering agree on its cause, only its effect: in a single night, the coastline the [[cartographers-guild]]'s founding charts recorded stopped matching the coastline that existed. Whole stretches of the [[ninth-vale]] region went under. Others surfaced where no chart had ever shown land.

The [[emberglass]] seams found along [[ashthorn-keep]]'s foundations date to this night. So, by most accounts, does the [[hollow-king]] — though no record from before the Sundering mentions him, and every record after treats his presence as simply a fact of the coast, the way the tide itself is a fact.

The [[order-ember]] rose to power on the promise of explaining, and ending, whatever caused it. It has not, in any account [[sera]] trusts, done either.
