The [[cartographers-guild]] has kept its working hall on the same rise above [[duskwater]]'s harbor for as long as anyone has bothered to record the guild's own history — one of the few advantages of a profession that literally keeps the records.

[[sera]] keeps a room above the hall for the nights the coast doesn't let her get home, and has redrawn more of the hall's own floor plan, in the guild's private jest, than she has redrawn the actual coastline. Every apprentice's first task is still copying a chart of the [[harbor-gate]] from the hall's reference wall, badly, until it stops being bad.
