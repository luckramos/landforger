## The keep that held, until it didn't

Ashthorn Keep was raised to watch the landward approach to [[duskwater]], back when the threat everyone planned for came by road instead of by tide. [[corin]] held its gate for eleven years, until the [[siege-duskwater]] ended with the gate open and Corin exiled for a door he swears he never touched.

:::callout {type="warning"}

The keep's east range has not been structurally sound since the drowning years. The [[cartographers-guild]] surveys it once a season regardless — someone keeps re-lighting the tower, and no one has volunteered to find out who.

:::

### Keep Hall

The great hall is the only wing still roofed. It hosts what passes, this turning of the tide, for a market when the [[salt-market]] floods — see [[keep-hall]].

### Keep Tower and Keep Court

The tower ([[keep-tower]]) is where the light keeps getting lit. The court below it ([[keep-court]]) is where Corin says he last saw the [[hollow-king]] stand still long enough to be counted.
