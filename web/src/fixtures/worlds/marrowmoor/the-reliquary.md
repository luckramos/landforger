No record from [[vigil-parish]] describes the Reliquary, because everyone who has actually stood before it has been someone who then stayed. [[mira-thorne]] tends it at the heart of [[the-heath]], and [[the-chapterhouse]] teaches — without ever having verified it — that the Keeper's vigil over the Reliquary is the whole reason [[the-hush]] has never lifted and never rolled further in.
