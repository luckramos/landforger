Mira Thorne has kept the reliquary at the heart of [[the-heath]] for longer than any parish record admits to counting, and every pilgrim the nearest chapterhouse has sent to relieve her of the duty has instead simply joined her at it, or not been heard from again. Neither outcome has ever been investigated with much enthusiasm.
