Vigil Parish is the last dry ground on the edge of [[the-heath]], and the Chapterhouse keeps its bells here so that a departing pilgrim can be rung out and, in theory, rung back in. The return peal has not been sounded in living memory. [[elin-marsh]] left from its gate; [[the-chapterhouse]] still lists the parish as her posting.
