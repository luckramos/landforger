Called at the base of [[the-spindle]] to settle the reading of [[the-founding-log]] once and for all, the session did the opposite. [[vann-toll]] stood and argued that a single agreed reason for [[the-departure]] would end the only project holding the ark together, and — to the surprise of everyone, [[keel-osei]] most of all — the council agreed. The Rememberers have preserved their contradictions on purpose ever since.
