The Rememberers keep their archive near the base of [[the-spindle]], where the oldest logs of [[the-departure]] still turn on their original reels. [[keel-osei]] leads them, though "leads" overstates it — the order has never agreed on a reason the ark left, and the elder Rememberers now argue that reconciling the logs would do more harm than the disagreement ever has. [[vann-toll]] is the loudest of them.
