Sternward Deck rides at the far end of [[the-spindle]]'s axis, where the gravity is thinnest and the logs of [[the-departure]] arrive last and most edited. Its people keep their own account of the voyage and resent every attempt to fold it into the archive's — which is why [[vann-toll]] is the deck's favourite Rememberer, and [[keel-osei]] its least.
