Vann Toll came up on [[sternward-deck]], farthest from the archive, and has spent a career inside [[the-rememberers]] arguing the opposite of everything [[keel-osei]] tries to do. Where Keel reconciles, Vann preserves the contradictions — and it was Vann who first stood up, at [[the-first-disagreement]], to say that agreeing on why the ark left would be the moment it finally stopped mattering that it had.
